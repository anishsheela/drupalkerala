About These Celebrations

In January 2026, the Kerala Drupal community came together to celebrate Drupal and Wikipedia's 25th anniversary with events in Thiruvananthapuram and Kochi. These celebrations brought together developers, enthusiasts, and contributors from across the state.

In the spirit of transparency and community accountability, we're publishing detailed financial reports for both events, including all income sources and itemized expenses.
